Liquid Nitrogen Dosage Calculator
LN₂ boils at −196 °C. 1 litre of LN₂ produces ~680 litres of N₂ gas. In a small enclosed space, oxygen displacement becomes a serious asphyxiation risk. Always work in a well-ventilated environment.

  • Open dewar valves slowly — pressure builds

  • Ensure all N₂ has evaporated before serving (30–60 s wait)
