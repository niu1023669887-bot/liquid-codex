Acid Adjustment Calculator
Every 0.5 g/L citric acid in water decreases pH by ~0.3 units (at pH 3–4). Malic acid is softer; tartaric is the most stable; lactic is creamy and round. Always adjust in small increments — 0.1 g/L at a time.

Pectinex Enzyme Clarification
Pectinex Ultra SP-L breaks pectin chains at 0.15–0.5 ml/L dosage, at 15–50 °C, within 1–12 hours. Higher temperature and dose accelerates clarification. Beyond 55 °C the enzyme denatures. Pre-chill after clarification to halt enzyme activity.

Centrifuge Balance Calculator
An unbalanced centrifuge rotor at 4000 rpm can destroy equipment and injure operators. Every tube must be balanced to ±0.1 g. Use a dummy tube with water to balance odd-numbered samples.
