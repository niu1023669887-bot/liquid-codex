SOP Protocol ▾
  1. Zest citrus using microplane — no white pith
  2. Combine peel and sugar in bowl; toss to coat
  3. Cover loosely; rest at room temp
  4. Stir every 30 min to redistribute sugar
  5. Strain; press peel firmly to extract final oils
  6. Add a squeeze of fresh juice to dissolve remaining sugar
Fat-Wash Calculator
Fat-washing dissolves lipophilic flavour compounds into spirits. The fat must be liquid at room temperature (infusion phase) and solid at 0 °C (separation phase). Optimal fat-to-spirit ratio: 50–150 ml fat per 750 ml spirit.

SOP Protocol ▾
  1. Melt / heat fat to liquid state
  2. Combine with spirit in sealed container; shake to emulsify
  3. Rest at room temperature for infusion time
  4. Transfer to freezer (−18 °C) until fat solidifies (4–6 h)
  5. Puncture fat cap; pour spirit through fine-mesh strainer
  6. Fine-strain through coffee filter to remove haze
Milk Clarification Calculator
Casein proteins precipitate tannins, pigments, and bitter phenolics when milk acidifies to pH 4.6. The resulting clarified spirit is silky, shelf-stable, and luminously clear.

SOP Protocol ▾
  1. Mix all non-milk ingredients; add citrus/acid first
  2. Slowly pour warm milk (35–40 °C) into acidic liquid
  3. Stir gently once; do not agitate further
  4. Rest 1–3 h until large curds form and settle
  5. Ladle clear liquid over muslin-lined funnel
  6. Final pass through Superbag or coffee filter
